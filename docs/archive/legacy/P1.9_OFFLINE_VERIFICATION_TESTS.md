### Test 2: Offline Chapter Editing - Autosave Queue

**Objective:** Verify chapter edits are queued and persisted while offline

**Steps:**
1. Ensure online connection and navigate to a chapter workspace
2. Verify autosave is working (look for "Saving..." → "Saved" indicators)
3. Disconnect network (DevTools or system)
4. Make 2-3 edits to chapter content (type text, delete text, etc.)
5. Wait 3-5 seconds (past autosave debounce window)
6. **Expected:** Queued autosave indicator appears (pending edits count shown)
7. Make 1-2 more edits
8. Reconnect network
9. **Expected:** Queued indicator clears, "Saving..." appears, then "Saved"
10. Refresh the chapter page (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R)
11. **Expected:** All offline edits are persisted (visible after reload)

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- ✅ Autosave queue indicator shows while offline
- ✅ Multiple offline edits are captured
- ✅ Most recent draft is used on reconnect (not oldest)
- ✅ Sync completes successfully
- ✅ Content persists after refresh
